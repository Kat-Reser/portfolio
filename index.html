Katja Madsen - Fullstack Developer

About

Katja immigrated from Denmark in 2004. with her then husband, who was a software developer, and three kids. She has always been fascinated about technology, and has wanted to learn how to code for some time. After doing a lot of research, and reading an article on Forbes about the top 9 online coding bootcamps, she decided to sign up with Altcademy.

In her spare time, Katja enjoys hiking with her dogs, or floating on the Arkansas river, if the waterlevels are high, but not too high. If the weather isn't for any of these activities, Katja enjoys practicing coding, using various websites, including freecodecamp.org, and khanacademy.org
